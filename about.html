About
DUNE Group

DUNE was founded in Singapore on 2 February 2009 with a single purpose: to provide maritime clients with reliable, integrated operational support across Southeast Asia.

For over a decade, we have built a group of four specialised companies, each purpose-built for its domain — shipping agency, marine services, supply & provisions, and offshore engineering.

We support shipowners, operators, and maritime professionals who need a partner that understands the technical demands of the industry, acts with urgency, and works with their priorities in mind.
